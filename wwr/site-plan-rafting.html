Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to offer families an experience of a lifetime. Break away from the hum-drum of your daily existence. Come experience a guided wilderness rafting adventure/ expedition that is life-changing, and family-friendly throughout the year(no previous experience needed). Our adventures/expeditions flow between the spectacular mountains of the Richtersveld and promises to be a fun-filled experience with beautiful, pristine scenery, wildlife, bird- and fish species, mind-blowing sunsets, tranquil sunrises, the call of the Fish Eagle, and good vibes.

Audience

1. Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on). My target audience is groups such as families, corporate team building, and adventurous nature lovers. 2. What do they want? My target audience are people wishing to break away from the mundane corporate lifestyle. They are the kind of people that love adventure and want to experience nature in a different way. 3. What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find information reagding all the different packages we offer from novice levels all the way up to the adrenelin junkies level. They will find contact information, pricing, directions, and special discounts on this site.
